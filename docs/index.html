Talk to Halo

A voice mode reachable from every tab. On-device speech recognition routes spoken commands to the right tracker — no network round-trip, nothing leaves your phone.

“Halo, add a to-do to call the dentist”
“Log a turkey sandwich”

Under the hood

Native, private, connected

Built the Apple way and wired into the system features you already use.

Siri & App Intents

Add to-dos, notes, and meals by voice without ever opening the app.

HealthKit

Dietary energy reads and writes keep your daily totals in sync with Apple Health.

WidgetKit

Home-screen widgets for upcoming to-dos and your daily calorie ring.

Shared App Group

App, widgets, and Siri intents all read one store — group.com.sachi.halo.

SwiftUI + SwiftData

A single ModelContainer behind a clean, glassy iOS 26 interface.

On-device only

Speech parsing and routing run locally. Your day stays on your phone.
