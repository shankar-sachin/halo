One command for everything

“Hey Siri, tell Halo that…”

…and Halo figures out the rest — no app to open, no tab to find. In the app, the waveform button in the tab bar opens Voice Mode from anywhere. On-device speech recognition routes spoken commands to the right tracker — no network round-trip, nothing leaves your phone.

What your voice can do

Control your life with just your voice

On-device AI, natural-language commands, and the native foundation that ties it together.

AI calorie & macros ✨ AI

Say what you ate in plain words — Halo's on-device Apple Intelligence model estimates the calories and protein/carbs/fat (no fixed food list, offline, private), counts down your budget, and syncs to Apple Health.

AI understanding ✨ AI

The same on-device model reads your intent, so natural phrasing just works — “I crushed a 5k this morning” logs a workout, “what's left for dinner?” answers a question.

One breath, many things ✨ AI

Stack commands in a single sentence — “I drank water and finished the dishes” — and Halo runs them all at once.

AI workout burn ✨ AI

“Log a 30 minute run” estimates the calories you burned on-device, so your activity actually counts toward your day.

AI meal suggestions ✨ AI

“What should I eat?” — Halo suggests meals that fit the calories you have left, tuned to your diet preferences and never an allergen.

AI weekly & monthly insights ✨ AI

“How was my week?” turns your 7-day trends into a plain-language insight and a practical tip; “how was my month?” reviews 30 days with a first-half/second-half trend — also right inside Home → Insights.

Cross-tracker patterns ✨ AI

An on-device correlation engine connects your day — “your mood averages 4.2 on workout days vs 3.1 on rest days” — with a sample guard so noise never becomes a trend.

End-of-day reflection ✨ AI

“Wrap up my day” writes a warm recap across every tracker, celebrating what went well and what to try tomorrow.

AI Sleep Coach ✨ AI

Tap Get sleep tips on the Sleep tab — Halo reads your last seven nights (your logs and Apple Watch sleep) and turns the numbers into one personalized, actionable tip.

Ask about the past

“What did I eat Tuesday?”, “how much water yesterday?”, “how did I sleep last week?” — answered with the exact figures for that day or week.

Proactive daily coach

An opt-in daily briefing nudge checks in on your whole day, at a time you choose.

AI mood journaling ✨ AI

Say how you feel — “stressed about the deadline” — and Halo logs the mood, writes a short reflective note, and replies with a kind word. A Reflect button in the Mood tab does the same for typed entries.

Notes → to-dos ✨ AI

“Pull to-dos from my last note” extracts the action items and creates them as tasks for you — or tap Extract To-Dos right in the note editor.

Ask Halo anything

“How many calories do I have left?”, “what's on my to-do list?”, “how much water today?” — Halo reads your data and answers out loud.

Daily briefing

“Halo, how's my day?” gives a one-breath recap — calories, water, open to-dos, and habit streaks across every tracker.

Edit & delete by voice

Not just create — “reschedule call mom to 7pm”, “rename groceries to buy oat milk”, “delete my last water” all work hands-free.

Hands-free

Turn on background listening and just say “Halo, …” while the app's open or pocketed — it confirms with a notification.

Siri & Shortcuts

Every tracker has a dedicated App Intent — Weight, Sleep, and Pills included — so all of Halo works from Siri and composes into your own Shortcuts automations.

In-app voice

Prefer to keep it in the app? Tap the waveform button in the tab bar and speak a whole sentence — the orb’s bars dance while Halo listens, and on-device recognition logs it to the right tracker, no Siri needed.

HealthKit

Dietary energy and weight read & write; Apple Watch workouts, weight, and sleep read back in; steps and active energy feed the Home Activity card — all in sync with Apple Health.

Today dashboard

A Home tab sums up every tracker at a glance — calorie ring, water, habits, to-dos, mood, workouts, pills, weight, sleep, and an Activity card with today’s steps and active energy — each a tap away.

Control Center & Lock Screen

One-tap controls to log a glass of water, complete a habit, mark a pill taken, or talk to Halo — right from Control Center.

Dynamic Island

Start a live workout and watch the timer on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island; finish to log it with an estimated burn.

Medication schedules

Set recurring reminders for your pills and supplements, and track today's adherence at a glance.

Rich-text notes

Format notes with bold, italic, and headings in the native iOS 26 editor.

WidgetKit

Home-screen widgets for upcoming to-dos, your daily calorie ring, and your habit streak — on the Home and Lock Screens.

Shared App Group

App, widgets, and Siri intents all read one store — group.com.sachi.halo.

SwiftUI + SwiftData

A single ModelContainer behind a clean, glassy iOS 26 interface.

On-device only

Speech parsing and routing run locally. Your day stays on your phone.
